About PDBA

Who Are We?
A bunch of players and patrons of a game we are passionate about.
What Are We?
A charitable association that uses all funds collected entirely for basketball promotion, coaching, events, and infrastructure — with zero profit motive.
🌱
An association which promotes basketball in the district by providing a platform for children, youth, and masters to express themselves through the medium of basketball.

❤️
A charitable association which uses all funds collected from its activities entirely for the above purposes — without any profit motive.
The Palghar District Basketball Association (PDBA) is dedicated to fostering the growth of basketball across Palghar district. Our mission is to inspire and develop players of all ages and skill levels through structured programs, events, and community initiatives. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced player, we invite you to join us in cultivating a vibrant basketball community.
⚡ What We Offer

Our Key Services

The PDBA organises a variety of programs to cater to different aspects of the sport.

🏋️
Professional Training & Coaching
Skill development sessions for individuals and teams, led by qualified and experienced PDBA coaches.
🏆
Local Tournaments
Regularly organised competitions across age groups to give players meaningful competitive exposure.
📋
Workshops & Clinics
Specialised camps focusing on specific skills, game strategies, and overall basketball IQ.
🌱
Youth Programs
Initiatives designed to introduce young talent to the sport and build a strong foundation from an early age.
🤝
Community Events
Activities aimed at building a vibrant, inclusive, and strong local basketball community across Palghar district.
